Corpora of the Tundra Nenets language

During the project, we collected both published and unpublished sources in the Tundra Nenets and Forest Nenets languages. These materials were subsequently digitised.

The metadata associated with these sources were systematically organised into a catalogue. Each metadata category was separated into individual columns to ensure clarity and facilitate efficient data organisation and analysis. We maintained consistent data formats across all fields, such as using a uniform date format (e.g., YYYY-MM-DD) and standardising categorical values, to ensure consistency and ease of processing.
